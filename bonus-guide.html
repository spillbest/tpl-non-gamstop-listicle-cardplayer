Types of Casino Bonuses

Licensed operators offer a small set of recurring bonus structures. Knowing the categories makes the terms predictable. Lorem ipsum dolor sit amet, consectetur adipiscing elit, sed do eiusmod tempor.

  • Deposit match: percentage added on top of a deposit, capped at a maximum
  • Free spins: a fixed number of spins on selected slots
  • No-deposit credit: small amount granted after verification, heavy conversion limits
  • Cashback: partial return of net losses over a defined period
  • Reload: recurring smaller match on later deposits

Reload and Cashback Offers

Validity Periods, Eligible Games and Bet Limits

Three further clauses determine whether a requirement is realistically completable. Temporibus autem quibusdam et aut officiis debitis aut rerum.

  • Validity: 7–30 days is typical; short windows make large requirements impractical
  • Game contribution: slots usually count 100%, table and live games 0–10%
  • Max bet: commonly €5 per spin or hand while wagering — exceeding it can void the bonus
  • Excluded titles: some high-RTP games do not contribute at all

How to Compare Offers Objectively

Compare offers by required turnover and constraints rather than headline size. A consistent checklist removes the marketing from the decision.

  • Compute required turnover: bonus × wagering multiplier
  • Divide turnover by validity days — is the daily volume realistic for your stakes?
  • Check contribution rates for the games you actually play
  • Note the max bet and conversion caps
  • If any clause is unclear, ask support before opting in — or decline the bonus

FAQs

Can I withdraw a bonus immediately?
No — bonus funds become withdrawable only after the wagering requirement is met within the validity period. Your own deposited funds normally remain withdrawable, though doing so may forfeit the bonus.
Do I have to accept the welcome bonus?
No. Every licensed operator in our ranking lets you decline the bonus at registration or in the cashier. Playing without a bonus means no wagering constraints apply.
What happens if a bonus expires?
Remaining bonus funds and any winnings derived from them are removed. Your own deposit balance is unaffected.
