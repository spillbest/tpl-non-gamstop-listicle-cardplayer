Why We Do Things This Way

Gambling content has a structural conflict: most comparison sites are paid by the operators they rank. We manage that conflict with fixed methodology, named accountability and public disclosure rather than pretending it does not exist. Commissions may be earned when readers visit an operator through our links — the weighting table above is what determines positions, and it does not include revenue. Fact Check

Before publication and at every update, a second editor independently re-verifies the claims in each article. The fact-check covers licence numbers against official registers, quoted bonus terms against the operator's current pages, and every statistic against its primary source.

  • Licence numbers re-checked in the regulator's public register
  • Quoted terms compared against the operator's live T&Cs
  • Regulatory citations traced to official bulletins
  • Corrections logged with date and description in the article's update note
